Club Activities

Club Activities

We are a cybersecurity club focused on competitions, learning, and community involvement. Some things we do include industry talks. We often have industry professionals talk to our club and inform us on the latest in cyber security.

Competitions

At Cyber@Stout we take competition very seriously. We compete in several industry competitions. These being CCDC, CPTC, NCL, NCAE, with more in the works. Our skills can be seen above at the CCDC Midwest Regionals Competition.

Fun

We as a club also love to have fun. Throughout the year we have fun events such as our cookouts, red team nights, open tool nights and more. Cookouts take place at the start of the academic year and at the end of year.

Stay Connected

You can follow us on social media to stay informed about upcoming events or opportunities. Make sure to join our Discord whether you are a Stout student or not!
