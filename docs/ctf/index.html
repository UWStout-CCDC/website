Capture The Flag

Cyber@Stout hosts a Capture The Flag (CTF) competition every semester. CTFs are a fun way to learn and practice cybersecurity skills in a competitive environment. Participants solve challenges that simulate real-world security scenarios, ranging from web vulnerabilities to cryptography.

Our CTFs are open to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. We provide resources and support to help you learn and improve your skills. Join us for an exciting and educational experience!

Learn to Hack

To help you prepare for our CTFs, we have compiled a list of resources and tools that can assist you in learning and practicing cybersecurity skills:

  • TryHackMe - Interactive cybersecurity training platform with hands-on labs.
  • Hack The Box - Online platform to test and advance your skills in penetration testing.
  • OverTheWire - Wargames to practice various security concepts.
  • picoCTF - Educational CTF platform for beginners.

We also recommend joining our Discord server where you can find teammates, ask questions, and share knowledge with the community.
